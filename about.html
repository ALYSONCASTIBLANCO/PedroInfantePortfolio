About me

I was Pedro Infante, a Mexican singer and actor born in 1917 in Sinaloa. I grew up in a humble family, where music became my passion from an early age. I became famous during the Golden Age of Mexican cinema, starring in films that represented everyday people and their struggles.

I sang rancheras and boleros with deep emotion, connecting with audiences through honest stories of love, sacrifice, and hope. Movies like Nosotros los pobres and Tizoc made me a symbol of Mexican culture. I died in 1957 in a plane crash, but my music and films continue to live on.
